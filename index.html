Why is Empowering Students for Success Essential?

Investing in our students is investing in the future of the American economy and the nation's social health. Our government has put policies in place that do not allow students to borrow money at the same rate as the big banks do when they borrow money from the Federal Reserve -- yet these banks are doing incredibly well. This is plain wrong. In addition, the cost of college has gone way up, making it more difficult for many families to afford a postsecondary education. This is particularly painful for those low- or moderate-income families who use education as a way to climb up the ladder and achieve greater economic and personal security and success. In addition, recent reports, studies and surveys show that, as a result, young Americans are putting off buying homes, starting families or opening businesses due to crippling student loan debt. While unemployment in Wisconsin is at 8% low (8%), 40% of families are still struggling to choose which bills they have to pay.
